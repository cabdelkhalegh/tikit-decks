Our Approach

The Big Idea: FRESH DROP

"Fresh Drop" borrows from sneaker and streetwear drop culture — something Saudi Gen Z deeply understands.

The "Drop" Works Triple

  • Product Drop — new Mentos SKU launches land like coveted releases
  • Beat Drop — music and content culture tie-ins
  • Fresh Drop — the moment you pop a Mentos and your vibe shifts
فريش دروب ALWAYS-ON DRUMBEAT

Why It Works for Saudi Gen Z

  • Drop culture = status, being first, social currency
  • "What's dropping next?" keeps Mentos in the feed
  • Authentically Saudi — cannot be replicated elsewhere
  • Connects on-ground moments to digital virality
  • Every month has a reason to talk about Mentos
